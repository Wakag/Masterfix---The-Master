Report a Maintenance Fault
1. Enter Maintenance Issue Title
• In the "Maintenance Issue" field, provide a brief title for the issue.
• Example: "Leaking pipe in bathroom" or "Broken window in common room."

2. Select the Problem Category
• Use the dropdown menu under "Problem Category" to select the type of maintenance issue.
• Categories may include:
    • General Maintenance: For common issues like broken furniture or lighting problems.
    • Electrical: For issues involving electrical wiring or power outages.
    • Plumbing: For water-related problems like leaks or blockages.
    • Other: For any issues that don’t fall under a specific category.

3. Provide a Detailed Description
• In the "Description" field, provide a short explanation of the problem.
• Be as detailed as possible so the maintenance team understands the issue.
• Example: "The sink in Room 12B is leaking water continuously and causing a puddle on the floor."

4. Upload a Picture (Optional)
• If possible, upload a picture of the issue. This can help the maintenance team better understand the problem.
• Click "Choose File" to browse and upload an image from your device.

5. Submit the Form
• After filling in all the details, click the **Submit** button.
• You will receive a confirmation that your requisition has been submitted successfully, and you can track the status of your request from your dashboard.
Information about Requisitions on the Dashboard
• You can view and track the status of your maintenance requests on the dashboard.

• Tracking Maintenance Status
    • Resolved: Once the issue has been fixed, it will be moved to the "Resolved" tab.
    • Pending/Other: Tickets that are awaiting action or are currently being addressed.

Note: If you have not seen an update for an extended period, you may contact your residence warden for assistance.
How to Check the Status of Your Maintenance Ticket
• Tickets can have the following statuses:
    • Requisition: Represents maintenance issues that have been requested.
    • Open: Faults that are currently in progress.
    • Confirmed: Faults that have been confirmed for action.
    • Resolved: Faults that have been fixed but not yet closed.
    • Closed: Maintenance faults that are fully resolved and completed.

• To track the status of your maintenance requests, view the dashboard and check the corresponding cards and pie chart.
• If there’s no update for an extended period, contact your residence warden for further assistance.
