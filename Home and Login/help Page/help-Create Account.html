Create an account
1. Select a Role
    • Role: Select your role from the dropdown:
    • Maintenance Staff
    • Student
    • House Warden

After completing all fields, click "Create Account" to submit.
2. Enter Your Personal Information
    • First Name: Type your first name in the "First Name" field
     Example: Lerato
    • Last Name: Type your last name in the "Last Name" field
     Example: Dlamini

Note: The details you input here should match the institution's database.
3. Provide Your Student ID
    • Student ID: Enter your unique student identification number.
     Example: gxxn4903

Note: Ensure this matches your institution's record.
4. Resident Information
    • Resident: Select your residence from the listed residences.
    • Room Number: Enter your room number.
     Example: Room 123
5. Log-in Details
    • Username: Choose a unique username for your account.
    • Password: Create a secure password with letters, numbers, and special characters.
     Example: P@ssword123

Note: Store your login details securely.
